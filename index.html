SCANAD Kenya

SCANAD Kenya is the flagship agency of marketing services group Scangroup. The HQ agency of SCANAD situated in Nairobi has a footprint in 5 countries in Sub Saharan Africa. SCANAD Kenya is responsible for managing the strategy, creative development, design, and communication planning for global and local businesses. With an in-house digital team working on brands across the SCANAD markets, SCANAD is able to provide holistic communication solutions to our clients. The brains behind all the communication is spearheaded by the Strategy and comms planning team out of Nairobi working closely with the local opcos to deliver locally relevant cultural insights which gives the end result the extra edge to resonate in the respective market.
